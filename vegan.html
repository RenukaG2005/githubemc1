Vegan & Vegetarian - World

The menus at these winning spots are plant-based, vegan, or vegetarian friendly, but that’s not even the headline—the food would stand out in any category (it’s that good).

The Travellers’ Choice Awards Best of the Best title celebrates the highest level of excellence in travel. It’s awarded to those who receive a high volume of above-and-beyond reviews and opinions from the Tripadvisor community over a 12-month period. Out of our 8 million listings, fewer than 1% achieve this milestone.

01. Chia Vegan Kitchen

Cusco, Peru

Enjoy a relaxed meal with good vibes and even better food at Chia Vegan Kitchen. The homey decor and friendly staff welcome you as you walk in, and the menu features fresh vegan dishes served with care. If you're looking for where to start, diners say you have to try the jackfruit tacos and beetroot juice.

02. Veggie Pause

Fez, Morocco

Hidden in the heart of an authentic medina, Veggie Pause offers creative dishes influenced by Moroccan flavours. It's a great spot for brunch (all meals really), with a charming, welcoming vibe and fresh, tasty food. Other diners recommend you try their seasonal veggie tagine with a freshly squeezed juice.

03. Blu Bar

Barcelona, Spain

Blu Bar is the spot you go when you want to have a good time with friends. Share their tapas-style dishes, or try their homemade pizzas and classic sandwiches—and wash it all down with one of their signature cocktails. There's lots of healthy options too, like salads and vegan dishes.

04. Dapur Usada

Ubud, Indonesia

Sample Indo-Vedic-inspired food at Dapur Usada. The cosy restaurant features an open kitchen and friendly, welcoming staff. You'll find lots of unique flavours and fresh, sustainable ingredients on the menu (the Ayurvedic bowls are a must-try). It's a great chance to try something new and get a healthy meal out of it.

05. Time Traveller

Bengaluru, India

Time Traveller offers a unique dining experience with its live cooking stations and diverse menu. The ambiance is warm and inviting, making it a great spot for family gatherings. Try the exclusive vegetarian buffet for a fresh and tasty selection. The staff is courteous and attentive, ensuring you have a pleasant experience. Don't miss the exceptional desserts—they're a real treat!
