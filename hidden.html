Hidden Gems - World

You have to get a bit off the beaten path to find these spots, but they certainly earn their winner status in both taste and local.

The Travellers’ Choice Awards Best of the Best title celebrates the highest level of excellence in travel. It’s awarded to those who receive a high volume of above-and-beyond reviews and opinions from the Tripadvisor community over a 12-month period. Out of our 8 million listings, fewer than 1% achieve this milestone.



01. Ponchos Food & Wines

Aguas Calientes, Peru

It's hard to come by views and food this good, but Ponchos Food & Wines serves up both, with picturesque views all around and beautifully-presented Peruvian dishes. They use fresh, local ingredients for a memorable meal. The staff is warm and welcoming, making you feel right at home.

02. Ely's Kitchen Ubud

Ubud, Indonesia

Ely's Kitchen Ubud is the perfect spot for a laid-back yet memorable dining experience. Their diverse menu features fresh produce and tantalizing spices, making every dish a delight. Travelers say their drinks are just as sensational as the food too. Psst...don’t miss the live entertainment—they’ve got some great musicians.

03. Bodega Biarritz

Barcelona, Spain

When you're in the mood for tapas, Bodega Biarritz is the place to be. This charming restaurant is well-known for its intimate setting and traditional Catalan dishes. Best part? Food and drinks here are reasonably priced and come with generous portions—a treat for travelers who love a great deal.

04. Lavang

Solihull, United Kingdom

Cozy yet modern, Lavang serves some of the best Indian food you’ll find. The passionate staff can help guide you through the menu if you’re not sure what to order. The restaurant is BYOB, so bring a bottle of your favorite wine and tuck into a bowl of delicious curry for a special evening.

05. Mulli Steak & Wine

Cusco, Peru

For authentic tastes in Cusco, try Mulli Steak & Wine. It's a local spot that features classic Peruvian dishes with a twist, like their signature steak (pair it with a glass of wine, of course). But what really sets it apart is the live music, local art, and views overlooking the city.
